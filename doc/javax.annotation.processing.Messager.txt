*javax.annotation.processing.Messager_Description*

AMessagerprovides the way for an annotation processor to report error messages, warnings, and other notices. Elements, 
annotations, and annotation values can be passed to provide a location hint for the message. However, such location hints may 
be unavailable or only approximate. 

Printing a message with an error kind(|javax.tools.Diagnostic.Kind|) will raise an 
error(|javax.annotation.processing.RoundEnvironment|) . 

Note that the messages printed by methods in this interface may or may not appear as textual output to a location like 
(|java.lang.System|) or (|java.lang.System|) . Implementations may choose to present this information in a different fashion, 
such as messages in a window.
