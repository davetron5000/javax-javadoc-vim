*javax.management.relation.Role_Description*

Represents a role: includes a role name and referenced MBeans (via their ObjectNames). The role value is always represented as 
an ArrayList collection (of ObjectNames) to homogenize the access. 

The serialVersionUID of this class is -279985518429862552L.
