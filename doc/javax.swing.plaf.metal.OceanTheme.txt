*javax.swing.plaf.metal.OceanTheme_Description*

The default theme for theMetalLookAndFeel. 

The designers of the Metal Look and Feel strive to keep the default look up to date, possibly through the use of new themes in 
the future. Therefore, developers should only use this class directly when they wish to customize the "Ocean" look, or force it 
to be the current theme, regardless of future updates. 

All colors returned byOceanThemeare completely opaque.
